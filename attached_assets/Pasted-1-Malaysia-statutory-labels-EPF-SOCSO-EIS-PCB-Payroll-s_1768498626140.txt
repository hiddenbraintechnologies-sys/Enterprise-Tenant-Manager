3️⃣ Razorpay Malaysia mapping (spec: subscriptions, trial, add-ons)
A) Business rules (recommended)

Payroll is an Add-on subscription item (separate plan) OR combined plan (plan+payroll bundle price)

For Malaysia, you want:

7-day trial for Payroll add-on

Charge only after trial ends

Plan upgrades: immediate

Plan downgrades: month-end

Payroll add-on removal: month-end (or immediate revoke access but keep data)

B) Razorpay objects you’ll likely use

Option 1: Razorpay Subscriptions (best for recurring)

Create Razorpay “Plan” objects for:

Core plans: MY_FREE, MY_BASIC, MY_PRO

Payroll tiers: MY_PAYROLL_25, MY_PAYROLL_100, MY_PAYROLL_UNLIMITED

Create a Subscription for core plan

Create another Subscription for payroll add-on (with start_at after trial OR trial via offer/custom flow)

Option 2: Razorpay Orders + internal recurring (not recommended)

More complexity; only do if subscriptions not available in your Razorpay account setup.

C) Trial handling (clean + controllable)

Recommended approach

In your DB: store payrollTrialEndsAt, payrollStatus=TRIALING

On add-on enable:

Immediately unlock payroll access

Schedule billing creation on trial end:

either create Razorpay subscription at that time

or create subscription now with start_at = trial end

If payment fails after trial:

payroll becomes PAST_DUE, show banner + restrict actions

D) Webhooks you MUST implement

subscription.activated

subscription.charged

invoice.paid

payment.failed

subscription.paused / cancelled (if supported)

subscription.completed

E) What you store in your DB per tenant

coreSubscriptionId, corePlanCode, coreStatus

payrollSubscriptionId (optional), payrollTier, payrollStatus

trialEndsAt (payroll)

nextBillingAt and cancelAtPeriodEnd flags

lastPaymentId, lastInvoiceId
